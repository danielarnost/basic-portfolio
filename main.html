About Me

Hi my name is Daniel and I am originally from the Boston area. I went to the University of Massachetts, Amherst and after graduating, I moved to California to work at a ski resort for a couple seasons. I had some amazing snowboarding seasons with an incredible amount of snow. Unfortunately they haven't had much snow accumulation since I moved on. I then attended graduate school at the University of Texas at Austin and recieved an M.S. in Geosciences.


My goal is to combine my advanced science degree with web development to help the general public understand scientific data. I would love to do web development for a company involved in science, but my future is wide open.
